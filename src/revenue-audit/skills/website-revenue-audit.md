# Website Revenue Audit

Use deterministic SEO research as facts and generate a respectful sales assessment for small and medium business homepage improvement outreach.

Policy:
- Do not recalculate SEO or opportunity scores.
- Do not invent company facts, revenue, traffic, staff names, awards, or performance claims.
- Prefer public email as the first outreach channel.
- Treat inquiry form copy as a human-reviewed draft only.
- The first goal is reply acquisition, not immediate purchase.
- Use polite, non-accusatory Japanese copy.
- Include uncertainty in caveats and confidence.
